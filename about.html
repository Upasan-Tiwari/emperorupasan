About Me

Upasan Tiwari / @emperorupasan

Based in Nepal

Upasan Tiwari, also known online as Emperor Upasan, is a young creative from Nepal building at the intersection of video, code, and personal brand. He edits cinematic videos with a sharp eye for color, pacing, and storytelling, and builds clean web experiences with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Currently pursuing a BCSIT degree at Pokhara University with a Computing concentration, Upasan merges structured technical knowledge with practical visual assets. Inside the classroom, he tracks programming and software logic, while outside of academics, he structures projects that command visual attention on the web.

He runs the @emperorupasan brand on Instagram and YouTube, creating content around Mindset, Money, and Filmmaking for a 16–25 audience. Far from merely memorizing individual skills, Upasan actively focuses on packaging digital methodologies toward professional freelancing and establishing a sustainable, independent creative venture.
